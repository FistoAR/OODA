INFO ABOUT

OUR PRODUCT

OODA BOND TG 109 thermal conducting Grease is a high temperature and high thermally conductive paste product. They are specially formulated for permanent and temporary bonding of thermocouples, thin film RTDs, thermistors and other temperature sensors, to most surfaces-metals, ceramics, glass, plastics, paper products. These are good adhesion and strength, high temperature rating, high thermal conduction, high electric insulation, thixotropic consistency, fast cure, and easy application. This Grease paste lowers thermal resistance to maximize performance and offers perfect wet-out performance in comparison to other brands products.

Application

Thermal Grease is a high-performance, thermally conductive compound engineered for efficient heat transfer between components and heat sinks, ensuring reliable performance in electronic, automotive, and industrial systems.
